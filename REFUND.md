# Refund Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-22_

## v0.9 Early Preview — no purchases, no refunds needed

The current v0.9 release is a **free 7-day Early Preview**. No payment is collected, no license is sold, so no refund process applies to this release.

If you dropped an optional tip on [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/korokokokstudio) and want it refunded, contact Ko-fi support or reply to your Ko-fi receipt — Ko-fi handles those under their own policy. I'll also happily honor any reasonable tip refund request at [hello@korokokok.app](mailto:hello@korokokok.app) if Ko-fi's flow is unhelpful.

## v1.0+ Refund Policy (applies once the commercial release ships)

Once Korokokok v1.0 launches with paid licensing, the following refund policy will apply to purchases:

### Try the free trial first

Korokokok will continue to offer a **free 7-day trial** with all features unlocked. We strongly encourage trying the software before purchasing — the trial is specifically designed to let you verify the app works on your hardware and fits your workflow.

### 14-day refund window

If you purchase a license and are not satisfied, you may request a refund within **14 days of purchase**.

### When refunds apply

Refunds will be granted for:
- The software not working on your system despite meeting the stated requirements
- Critical bugs that prevent you from completing core tasks
- Purchase made by mistake (duplicate order, wrong product)

### When refunds do not apply

Refunds will not be available for:
- "Changed my mind" requests after 14 days
- Hardware that does not meet the published minimum requirements (see [README](README.md#system-requirements))
- Inability to run the software due to modifications, incompatible third-party software, or user error
- AI-generated outputs not matching your expectations (results vary by prompt, hardware, and model)

### How to request a refund (v1.0+)

Once paid licensing is live, refund requests will go through the payment provider (Lemon Squeezy) — reply to your receipt email with a brief reason and confirmation that you want the license deactivated. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

## Questions Before Buying

Not sure if Korokokok will work for you? Install the **free 7-day v0.9 Early Preview** first — zero risk, no payment, full features. If you still have questions, email [hello@korokokok.app](mailto:hello@korokokok.app) and I'll answer honestly before you commit to the paid release.
